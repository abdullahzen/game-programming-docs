# Playtest

Rules:
* Participating in the playtest session assigned to you is mandatory and it is a graded activity.
* NDA document must be signed electronically using Adobe Acrobat PDF, and submitted on the NDA document dropbox on Omnivox (Lea). The NDA document is required to obtain a copy of the game and to protect the developers of the game in case they want to publish it later on. 
* You are expected to play every feature of the game and try to break the game, if possible.
* Duration of playtest is 1 hour and a half, and then an additional 15 minutes are needed to fill up the playtest survey. 
* The survey is required by the end of your assigned playtest session.

> **One member** of the game owner team is required to be there on the day their game is being played (Check the table & example below) to make sure everything is running smoothly or to intervene in case the game does not start.   

<br>

## Example
On the 4:00pm Monday class, Team 1 will playtest Project Gunball. 
One member of Project Gunball (Team 4) is expected to attend that playtest session to ensure everything is running smoothly.
